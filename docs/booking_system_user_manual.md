# User Manual: Counseling Booking System

Welcome to the Clinical Management Portal's Intelligent Booking System. This system is designed to make scheduling easy, conflict-free, and healthy for our clinical team.

## 👨‍🏫 For Counselors: Managing Your Availability

Your availability is the foundation of the booking system. By setting your hours accurately, you ensure that students can only book you when you are actually ready for sessions.

### 1. Setting Up Your Working Hours
1.  Navigate to the **Availability Settings** page from your sidebar.
2.  For each day of the week (Monday - Friday), enter your **Start Time** and **End Time**.
3.  Use the **Active Toggle** to completely block off days where you are not taking appointments (e.g., if you are off every Wednesday).
4.  Click **Save Changes** to update the system instantly.

### 2. Understanding the 30-Minute Buffer
The system automatically adds a mandatory **30-minute gap** after every session you complete.
- **Example**: if a student books you from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM, the system will mark you as "Busy" until 10:30 AM.
- This gives you time for notes, breaks, or sessions that might run slightly over.

## 👩‍🎓 For Students: Booking a Session

Booking a session is a simple 3-step process designed to get you the support you need at a time that works.

### 1. Select Your Specialist
- On the **Book Session** page, browse through our counselors. You can see their titles and specialties (e.g., Dr. Miller - Lead Clinical Psychologist).
- Click **Book Now** on the counselor you'd like to meet with.

### 2. Configure Your Session
In the booking window, you will:
- **Choose a Date**: Select the day you'd like to visit.
- **Choose Mode**: Select either a **Virtual Session** (Video call) or **In-Person** (At the clinic).
- **Select Duration**: You can choose between **30, 60, 90, or 120 minutes**. 
  - *Note: Longer sessions will have fewer starting times available, as the system must ensure the counselor is free for the entire block plus the buffer.*

### 3. Choose your Starting Time
- The system will show you only the available starting times for your chosen duration.
- Select a time that fits your schedule and click **Complete Booking**.

### 🛠️ Frequently Asked Questions
- **Why can't I book a 2-hour session?** 
  If the counselor only has a 1.5-hour gap left in their day, a 2-hour session will not be shown as an option.
- **What happens if I'm late?**
  Counselors typically wait for 10-15 minutes. The 30-minute buffer helps manage small delays, but try to be on time!
